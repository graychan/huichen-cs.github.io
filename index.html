Hui Chen, Ph.D. Associate Professor of Computer Science @ Virginia State University

Biographical Sketch

Hui Chen is on the computer science faculty, Virginia State University. His recent and current research is on a few problems in wireless networks, wireless sensor networks, and system and network security. He currently teaches courses in computer networks, computer security, and web programming among others. He is a member of IEEE and ACM. He served and has been serving on technical program committees of conferences sponsored by IEEE or ACM.
