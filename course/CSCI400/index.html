Overview

Discussions based on current researches that are facilitated by students, faculty members, or in- dustry representatives. Students are expected to facilitate the discussion at least once during the semester. The impact of the research in a global and societal context will be discussed.

Prerequisites: Senior level standing in Computer Science or consent of Instructor
